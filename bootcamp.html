Got questions?

We encourage you to ask questions before you make up your mind. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to drop a line at hey@peoplewho.build!

What is my ROI after completing this bootcamp?

After you're done with the bootcamp, you can build customisable software, websites & web applications for both desktop & mobile browsers. Examples include but are not limited to - social media platforms, SaaS products, eCommerce marketplaces & internal tools like inventory management systems.

Each & every student who utilised what we taught in our last bootcamp (basics of No-Code), recovered their investment within one month of completing the course.

How much experience with No-Code do I need?

You can be new to NoCode and still build something great in 4 weeks! In fact, the majority of our students have just started their journey with No-Code.

Do I need an idea for an app?

You don't need a final idea, but we do recommend that you have a particular problem that you are trying to solve. From there, your Bubble instructor will help you refine and iterate during your 4 weeks together, as well as help you take in and synthesize user feedback.

Where do the classes take place?

All our classes are remote via Zoom! Students join our bootcamp from all over the country.

What is the time commitment?

Over the course of your 4 weeks, there will be 16 hours of instruction in addition to assignments given between classes. The assignments between classes will be where you do the majority of your building, so the more you put into these assignments, the more you will get out of your bootcamp experience.

What do I need to participate in the class?

All our classes take place remotely via Zoom so you just need a laptop/computer with a decent internet connection.

Are the sessions recorded?

Yes, all our sessions are recorded and bootcamp participants are given access to them to review between classes.

Do I get a certification after completing the bootcamp?

Yes! Upon passing this course, you will receive a signed certificate of completion.

Can I ask my employer to reimburse the course fee?

Yes! And to help you out with the reimbursement process - here's document you can read.

Who are we?

You might remember us from the Design community, PeopleWho.Design. We started a nocode school to empower startup founders, designers & students to build apps, web apps, websites, etc. - without code.

Who are the instructors?

For this Bootcamp, we have two instructors - Nazz & Mayank. Nazz built & sold India's first nocode startup, while Mayank co-founded India's first nocode school, PeopleWho.Build.

What tools will we be learning?

Although we prioritize the process over tools, we'll be using Bubble for this bootcamp. Since Bubble handles everything (frontend, backend, databases, memberships, etc.) in-house, it didn't make sense to introduce other tools in this bootcamp.
